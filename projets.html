Hotel Manager

Hotel manager is a managing app that let the user may he be client or staff member of a particular chain of hotel operate various task such as:

  • Client side
    • Creating a customer account
    • Searching applying diffrent combinations filters
    • Booking a room
  • Staff member side
    • Add or delete a new staff member
    • Manage booking requests
    • Access various informations about clients

The application was part of one of our project in a previous class. It runs on android and was coded using Java. It was also heavily based on data manipulation which was the more challenging part. We used PostgreSQL to manage the database part of the project.
